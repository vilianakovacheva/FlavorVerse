Pasta Bolognese

Pasta Bolognese is a classic Italian dish with a rich, meaty tomato sauce, served over pasta like tagliatelle or spaghetti. Perfect for a comforting and flavorful meal.

PREP TIME

15 MINS

COOK TIME

60 MINS

SERVINGS

4 PEOPLE

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup whole milk or heavy cream
  • 1 lb (450g) pasta (spaghetti, tagliatelle, or your choice)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion, carrot, and celery, and sauté until softened (about 5-7 minutes).
  2. Add garlic and cook for another 1 minute.
  3. Add the ground meat to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Pour in the wine (if using) and cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing it to reduce slightly.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer on low heat for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add milk or cream and stir to combine. Let it cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and reserve some pasta water.
  8. Toss the pasta with the sauce, adding a little pasta water if needed to combine.
  9. Serve with grated Parmesan, if desired.
